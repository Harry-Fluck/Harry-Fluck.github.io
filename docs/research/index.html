Research Interests

I study geometric analysis, with my primary interest being singularity formation for the Ricci flow. Recently, I have been working on questions surrounding the structure theory for a general non-collapsed limits of flows such as sharp Minkowski estimates. I am also interested in singularity formation for other geometric equations, and how the techniques used may be applied to Ricci flow.
